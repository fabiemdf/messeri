2025 Hurricane Season Forecast: What Florida Property Owners Need to Know

March 28, 2025
David Messeri
Hurricane Season

As we approach the 2025 hurricane season, Florida property owners need to be prepared for what meteorologists are predicting could be another active season. This article provides the latest forecast information and essential preparation steps to protect your property.

1. 2025 Hurricane Season Predictions

Early forecasts for the 2025 hurricane season indicate:

  • Above-average number of named storms
  • Increased likelihood of major hurricanes
  • Higher than normal sea surface temperatures
  • Potential for rapid intensification events
"The combination of warm ocean temperatures and favorable atmospheric conditions suggests an above-average hurricane season for 2025."

2. Property Preparation Checklist

Take these steps to prepare your property:

  • Review and update your insurance coverage
  • Document your property's current condition
  • Create a detailed inventory of possessions
  • Develop an emergency response plan

3. Insurance Coverage Review

Ensure your insurance coverage is adequate:

  • Check your policy limits and deductibles
  • Verify coverage for additional living expenses
  • Review flood insurance requirements
  • Understand your policy's exclusions

4. Emergency Preparedness

Create a comprehensive emergency plan:

  • Establish evacuation routes and procedures
  • Prepare emergency supply kits
  • Secure important documents
  • Develop a communication plan

5. Post-Storm Response

Know what to do after a storm:

  • Document all damage thoroughly
  • Contact your insurance company promptly
  • Take immediate steps to prevent further damage
  • Keep all receipts for emergency repairs
